Out Partners

At FossilSoft, collaboration is at the heart of everything we do. We are proud to work with leading organizations and technology providers that share our passion for science, technology, and discovery. Together, we push the boundaries of paleontological research and make our tools more impactful for the scientific community.

Esri - Leaders in GIS technology, helping us provide accurate excavation mapping and analysis.
