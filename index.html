Fossilz Corp.

At Fossilz Corp., we develop specialized software for paleontologists. Our tools help scientists organize fossil data, analyze excavation sites, and share discoveries with the research community. Our mission is simple: make the study of ancient life easier, smarter, and more accurate.

What Do We Use

At FossilSoft, we use modern tools to make paleontology smarter and easier. Python powers our data analysis, PostgreSQL stores fossil records, and GIS software maps excavation sites. With machine learning, 3D scanning, and AR/VR technology, we help researchers study fossils and share discoveries in innovative ways.
